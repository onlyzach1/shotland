Introducing Glo Vanity

It's the lighted mirror that lets you apply make-up, style hair, or tend to eyebrows in any room or on the go. The Glo Vanity mirror is made from distortion-free glass that is encircled by eight bright LEDs, providing clear, crisp reflections enhanced by bright white lights or simulated natural daylight.
